# The Dark Prison of the Gargoyle Countess
{{margin-top:25px}}
{{wide
##### The dungeon was created by dwarves as a stronghold. It was eventually conquered by invaders, and has been attacked and abandoned many times since then.
::
##### A randomly generated D&D 5e donjon dungeon for a party size of 6 and APL8
::::
##### Created using [Homebrewery](https://homebrewery.naturalcrit.com), [Donjon](https://donjon.bin.sh) and [donjon_to_homebrewery](https://github.com/telboy007/donjon_to_homebrewery)
}}

## Description
The dungeon has the following features, these may include skill checks to perform certain actions.
{{descriptive
#### General Features
| Type | Detail |
|:--|:--|
| Floors | Flagstone |
| Walls | Superior Masonry (DC 20 to climb) |
| Temperature | Warm |
| Lighting | Shadowy (phosphorescent fungus or candles every 20 ft.) |
| Special | None |
}}
Some of the corridors marked on the map have special features detailed below.
{{descriptive
#### Corridor Features
| Type | Detail |
|:--|:--|
| a | Poisoned Net Trap: DC 10 to find, DC 15 to disable;     affects all targets within a 10 ft. square area, DC 12 save or take 4d10 poison damage     and become restrained, half damage on a successful save |
| c | Ice Spear Trap: DC 15 to find, DC 15 to disable;     +6 to hit against one target, 4d10 cold damage |
| e | Rune of Paralyzation: DC 15 to find, DC 15 to disable;     affects all targets within 10 ft., DC 10 save or become paralyzed for 1d4 rounds |
| i | A group of demonic faces have been carved into the walls |
| m | An acrid odor fills the corridor |
| n | Patches of mushrooms grow here |
| r | Rusting iron spikes line the walls |
| s | A foul odor fills the corridor |
}}
